# Identity and Access Management (IAM) Standards

## 1. Device Enrollment Standards
* **Microsoft Entra Joined**: 
    - **Policy**: Mandatory for all company-purchased hardware.
    - **GRC Logic**: Provides full administrative control and policy enforcement for banking security compliance.
* **Microsoft Entra Registered**: 
    - **Policy**: Restricted to personal devices (BYOD) for basic application access only.

## 2. Authentication Protocol
> All employees must utilize Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) via Microsoft Authenticator to protect sensitive financial data.

## 3. Incident Lessons (SC-900 Unit 2)
* **Goal**: Ensure zero-trust access for company-issued assets.
* **Key Learning**: Confirmed that "Entra Joined" is the required state for devices purchased by the organization to ensure they are managed under the corporate identity.
